FAQs
Q: What is this app for?
A: This is an Android application that provides two portals, one for employers and one for employees, to manage employee attendance using facial recognition technology.
Q: How does the registration process work for employees?
A: Employees must register on the app using their organization-provided email. They need to submit a photo of their organization ID card, a self-portrait, and fill in all required fields such as name, employee number, etc
Q: How does the employer verify employee identity?
A: The employer can verify employee identity from the backend portal after the employee submits their registration information.
Q: How do employees mark their attendance?
A: Employees can mark their attendance by looking at the camera within a specific time slot at the organization's location.
Q: How is employee attendance marked?
A: Employee attendance is marked through facial recognition technology, and the attendance is listed in the database once it is done.
Q: What information is recorded for marked attendance?
A: Marked attendance includes employee entry and exit times along with all basic details.
Can employees check their attendance from anywhere?
A: Yes, employees can check their marked attendance from anywhere within the provided geo-location
Q: Who can download attendance information?
A: Only the employer can download attendance information at any time.
Q: What is the Todo List feature of this app?
A: The Todo List is a feature that allows employees to maintain their task completion and task schedule in a single place.
Q: Can employees prioritize their tasks on the Todo List?
A: Yes, employees can prioritize their tasks on the Todo List.
Q: Is the Todo List feature only accessible to employees?
A: Yes, the Todo List feature is only accessible to employees.
Q: What is the Event Page feature of this app?
A: The Event Page is a feature that informs employees about upcoming news, holidays, or important bulletins of the company.
Q: Is the Event Page feature customizable?
A: Yes, the Event Page feature can be customized according to the company's requirements.
Q: What is the Apply for Leave section feature of this app?
A: The Apply for Leave section is a feature that allows employees to directly apply for leave to their employer.
Q: Is the Apply for Leave section feature accessible to all employees?
A: Yes, the Apply for Leave section feature is accessible to all employees.
Q: What is the Streak section feature of this app?
A: The Streak section is a feature that maintains the streaks of the user for their attendance.
Q: Can employees track their attendance streaks using the Streak section?
A: Yes, employees can track their attendance streaks using the Streak section.
